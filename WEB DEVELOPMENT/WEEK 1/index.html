Text Formatting

Bold Text is used to emphasize important words.

Italic Text is used for emphasis or to indicate a title.

Underlined Text is often used for links or to highlight important information.

Highlighted Text is used to draw attention to specific parts of the text.

Small Text is used for less important information.

Deleted Text is used to indicate text that has been removed.

Inserted Text is used to indicate text that has been added.

Subscript Text is used for chemical formulas or mathematical expressions.

Superscript Text is used for exponents or footnotes.

Blockquotes

This is a blockquote. It is used to display a quote or a citation from another source.
